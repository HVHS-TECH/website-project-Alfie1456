Lexus

Lexus is a luxury brand known for its strong reliability, advanced safety features, and long-lasting build quality, making it a solid choice for a first car if bought used. It offers smooth performance, a comfortable interior, and many standard safety systems that help new drivers feel confident. However, Lexus vehicles can be more expensive to buy, insure, and maintain than non-luxury brands. Overall, a smaller or older Lexus can be a great first car for someone wanting comfort and dependability with a bit of luxury.
