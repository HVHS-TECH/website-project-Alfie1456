Station Wagon

Station wagons are practical first cars thanks to their spacious interiors and large cargo areas, making them ideal for families or carrying extra gear. They generally offer good fuel efficiency and a comfortable ride, often similar to sedans but with added versatility. Maintenance costs are usually reasonable, and parts are widely available for mos t models. Overall, a station wagon can be a smart first car choice for those who need space, comfort, and everyday reliability.
