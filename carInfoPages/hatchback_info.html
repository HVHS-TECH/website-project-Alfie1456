Hatchback

Hatchbacks are practical first cars thanks to their compact size, making them easy to park and maneuver in city driving. They usually offer good fuel efficiency and a versatile boot space that can handle both daily errands and weekend trips. Maintenance costs are generally reasonable, and parts are widely available, keeping running costs low. Overall, a hatchback can be a smart first car choice for beginners who want practicality, economy, and easy handling.
