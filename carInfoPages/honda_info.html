Honda

Honda is well known for being reliable and low maintenance , with annual repair and ownership costs generally well below average. It comes with good safety technology in many models, such as adaptive cruise control, collision mitigation, and lane-keeping assist, which helps new drivers feel more secure. While Hondas are typically more affordable than luxury brands, you'll still want to watch out for costs like insurance, fuel, and servicing on newer or high-spec models. Overall, a Honda—especially a compact or modestly powered one—is one of the safest and smartest choices for a first car.
